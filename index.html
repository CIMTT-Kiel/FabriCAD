FabriCAD Dataset

A Synthetic Dataset of Manufacturing Plans and corresponding CAD data

About the Dataset

FabriCAD is a fully synthetic dataset of detailed manufacturing process plans paired with corresponding CAD data. It is designed to support research in ai assisted manufacturing approaches.

  • Parametric CAD models (STEP)
  • Structured, multi-step process plans
  • Human-readable and machine-readable formats (csv)
  • Suitable for machine learning approaches
